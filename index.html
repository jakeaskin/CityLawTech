The goal of CivicLawTech is to help people work with government more effectively. CivicLawTech encompasses projects that are either legal or civic technology that involve the government as an end-user. This website seeks to serve as a landing page for CivicLawTech projects, with an index of projects characterized by different types, a calendar for upcoming events, and a map that works to place.
